Questions, answered

Everything you need to know before recycling with reValu.

Which materials can I recycle?

We support PET, HDPE, PP, paper, cardboard, metals, and glass. You will see accepted streams for your area inside the app.

How fast do rewards arrive?

Rewards are released instantly once collectors submit verified weights and photos at the depot.

Do I need special bins?

No. The app guides you on how to clean and bag items using materials you already have.

Is there a plan for enterprises?

Yes. We offer APIs, white-labeled apps, and ESG reporting tailored for corporates and property managers.
